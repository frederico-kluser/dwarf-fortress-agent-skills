# Armor stand

> Fonte: [Armor stand](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Armor_stand) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Armor stands** are a type of furniture. Much like the weapon rack, the armor stand can be used to create a barracks. After being built (-), you may define the barracks dimensions (-, then ). It can also be placed in a noble's room to appease them.

When used in a barracks designated for individual and squad equipment, dwarves will prefer to store their armor on stands instead of stockpiles. Doing so in concert with weapon racks will allow you to create centralized armories for your military.

In tombs designated for parasites nobles or military dwarves, armor stands will be used to store the deceased dwarf's armor.

Armor stands do not block dwarf movement.
