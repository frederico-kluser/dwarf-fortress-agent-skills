# Farmer's workshop

> Fonte: [Farmer's workshop](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Farmer's_workshop) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

The **Farmer's workshop** is the workshop used for any jobs involving processing plants or animal products. It is built from any construction material, and must be built by a dwarf with the Plant processing, Milking, Shearer, Spinner, or Cheese making labors enabled. Gelding also occurs at a farmer's workshop. This is not a selectable menu option: instead, any animals that are designated to be gelded will automatically be assigned a gelding job at a farmer's workshop.

## Menu

**Farmer's Workshop**

- ()  - make thread from plants (pig tails, rope reed, flax plants, jute plants, hemp plants, cotton plants, ramie plants, and kenaf plants)
- ()  - with an empty barrel, turn sweet pods into dwarven syrup & seeds
- ()  - with an empty vial, turn valley herbs into golden salve
- ()  - make cheese from any milk
- ()  - with an empty bucket, milk a milkable creature
- ()  - shear wool from alpacas, llamas and sheep (or tame troll)
- ()  - make thread from wool (see previous), or from hair from other creatures
- ()  - with an empty bag, process quarry bushes into leaves and rock nuts
- *(n/a)*  - make papyrus sheets from the papyrus sedge plant

If a then you lack the raw material to perform that task.

## Common Uses

- Turn plants into thread, for weaving at a loom.
- Turn certain plants into food and extracts
- Harvest milk and turn it into cheese.
