# Language SYM.txt/Death

> Fonte: [Language SYM.txt/Death](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Language_SYM.txt/Death) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

## Usage

Legendary beings, such as a night troll or nightmare, associated with the spheres of blight, death, disease, murder, nightmares, or suicide will have words from this symbol in their name.

Alongside EVIL, NEGATIVE, and UGLY, DEATH is one of the possible subsymbols available to evil biomes.

The slab of a necromancer is always created by a god of death, and as a divine object will reflect the spheres of its creator when being named. Depending on the god's portfolio, other symbols may be added to the list of potential words when naming the item.
