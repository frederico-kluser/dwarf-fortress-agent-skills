# No Exert

> Fonte: [No Exert](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/No_Exert) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

\ **No Exert** creatures (those with the token) need no sleep, and never become tired or exhausted. In the case of a particularly dangerous creature like a bronze colossus, the NOEXERT tag guarantees that the foe will continue the battle as long as there are any dwarves left alive. \

## Creatures who feel no exertion

The following creatures possess the token.

- Amethyst men
- Angels
- Blood men
- Bogeymen
- Bronze colossuses
- Demons
- Dragons
- Experiments
- Fire men
- Forgotten beasts
- Gabbro men
- Grimelings
- Hydras
- Infected ghouls
- Iron men
- Magma men
- Mud men
- Mummies
- Nightmares
- Night trolls
- Nightwings
- Undead
- Titans
- Vampires
- Werebeasts
