# Blue shark

> Fonte: [Blue shark](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Blue_shark) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Blue sharks** are cartilaginous fish that can be found in any ocean biome. As big as an elk and five times the size of an average dwarf, they are a large species of aquatic carnivore that produces a decent amount of meat when butchered. As fully-sized creatures, they can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with a drowning chamber. An infant blue shark is called a *blue shark pup*.

Being cartilaginous, blue sharks won't give bones if butchered (though they'll still provide a skull).

Some dwarves like blue sharks for their *coloration*.
