# Dwarf Fortress Wiki

> Fonte: [Dwarf Fortress Wiki](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Dwarf_Fortress_Wiki) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

The **Dwarf Fortress Wiki** is a free-access, free-content *Dwarf Fortress* wiki, supported and hosted by some guy called Briess Emi and Lethosor. Those who have created an account on the site can edit most of its articles. The Dwarf Fortress Wiki is ranked among the ten most popular *Dwarf Fortress*-related websites^(a), and constitutes the Internet's largest and most popular *Dwarf Fortress* reference^(b).

It launched on October 29, 2007, and has [](Special:Statistics) articles.

The hosting costs for the wiki have generously been provided by Kitfox Games since November 2022.

## History

### 40d era

Briess launched the Dwarf Fortress Wiki on October 29, 2007.

### v0.31 era

A new version of the wiki was cloned for the 2010 release. It appears that the 23a version of the wiki was retroactively created during this time.

### v0.34 era

A new version of the wiki was cloned for the 2012 release.

### DF2014 era

A new version of the wiki was cloned for the 2014 release. This is the most recent version.

## Operation

### Software operations and support

The operation of the Dwarf Fortress Wiki depends on MediaWiki, a custom-made, free and open source wiki software platform written in PHP and built upon the MySQL database system.

### Hardware operations and support
