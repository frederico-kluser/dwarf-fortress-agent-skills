# Blacktip reef shark

> Fonte: [Blacktip reef shark](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Blacktip_reef_shark) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Blacktip reef sharks** are cartilaginous fish that can be found in tropical oceans. At half the size of a dog, they pose no threat to dwarves. As fully-sized creatures, they can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with a drowning chamber. An infant blacktip reef shark is called a *blacktip reef shark pup*.

Being cartilaginous, blacktip reef sharks won't give bones if butchered (though they'll still provide a skull).

Some dwarves like blacktip reef sharks for their *curiosity* and their *distinctive dorsal fin*.
