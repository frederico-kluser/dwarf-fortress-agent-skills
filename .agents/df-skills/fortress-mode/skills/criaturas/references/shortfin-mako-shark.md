# Shortfin mako shark

> Fonte: [Shortfin mako shark](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Shortfin_mako_shark) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Shortfin mako sharks** are cartilaginous fish that can be found in temperate and tropical oceans. Slightly larger than the average dwarf, they provide a fair amount of meat when butchered. As fully-sized creatures, they can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with a drowning chamber. An infant shortfin mako shark is called a *shortfin mako shark pup*.

Being cartilaginous, shortfin mako sharks won't give bones if butchered (though they'll still provide a skull).

Some dwarves like shortfin mako sharks for their *speed*.
