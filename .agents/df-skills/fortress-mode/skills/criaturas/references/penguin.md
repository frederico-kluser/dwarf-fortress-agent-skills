# Penguin

> Fonte: [Penguin](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Penguin) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Penguins** are small creatures found inhabiting arctic oceans, where they spawn in groups of 5-10 individuals. Only as big as a blue peafowl, these tiny flightless birds are completely inoffensive, serving as little more than a distraction for a dwarf. They produce very few products when butcherered, making hunting them for food rather worthless, though they are arguably useful as target practice, at least.

Penguins possess a pet value of 10, but lack the necessary tokens to be trainable.

Some dwarves like penguins for their *coloration*, their *waddling gait*, and their *way of flying through the water*.
