# Plum

> Fonte: [Plum](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Plum) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Plum trees** are one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, plum wood is brown and produces brown products. Plums can be gathered in a gathering zone, and they can be brewed into plum wine, eaten raw, or cooked.

Some dwarves like plum trees for their *fruit*.
