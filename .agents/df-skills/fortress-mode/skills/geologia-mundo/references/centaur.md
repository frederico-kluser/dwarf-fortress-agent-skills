# Centaur

> Fonte: [Centaur](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Centaur) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Centaurs** are fanciful creatures which are merely the stuff of legends. They do not actually exist, appearing only in engravings as the fancy of artists. They may be added properly into the game in future releases. A female centaur is known as a *centauress*.

Some dwarves like centaurs for their *strength*.

Some trolls like centaurs for their milk.
