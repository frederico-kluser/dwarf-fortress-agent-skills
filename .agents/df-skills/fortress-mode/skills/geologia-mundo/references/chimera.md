# Chimera

> Fonte: [Chimera](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Chimera) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Chimeras** are fanciful creatures which are merely the stuff of legends. They do not actually exist, appearing only in engravings as the fancy of artists. They may be added properly into the game in future releases.

Some dwarves like chimeras for their *terrifying features*.

Chimeras are known for their uncanny ability to write stories that are intentionally poor to the point of humor. Their most famous works speak of warriors fighting the Hidden Fun Stuff and of researchers recovering unusual forms of smelted pitchblende.
