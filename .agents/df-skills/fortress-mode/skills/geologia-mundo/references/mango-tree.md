# Mango tree

> Fonte: [Mango tree](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Mango_tree) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Mango trees** are a type of above ground tree, found only in tropical forests. Mango wood is brown ("burnt sienna" as described in the raws) and produces brown products.

Mango trees bear **mangoes** that can be harvested by setting up a gathering zone. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into mango wine.

Some dwarves like mango trees for their *sweet-smelling flowers*.

Some human bosses insist that they will farm mangoes after one last job, and that the other bandits just need to trust them.
