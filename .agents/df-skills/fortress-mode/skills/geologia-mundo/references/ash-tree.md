# Ash (tree)

> Fonte: [Ash (tree)](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Ash_(tree)) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Ash trees** are one of the many genera of above ground trees modeled in *Dwarf Fortress*. Logs from cut ash trees are brown and produce brown products.

Constructions and other items made from ash wood are referred to as Ashen; e.g.: -Ashen Bed-. This is to distinguish them from constructions made from ashes.

Some dwarves like ashes for their *autumn coloration* and their *flying keys*.
