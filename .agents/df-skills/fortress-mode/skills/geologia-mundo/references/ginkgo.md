# Ginkgo

> Fonte: [Ginkgo](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Ginkgo) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Ginkgo** is one of the many *genera* of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, ginkgo wood is brown and produces brown products.

Ginkgo trees frequently have 2-tile-wide trunks. Ginkgo seeds can be harvested by setting up a plant gathering zone, and can be eaten or cooked.

Some dwarves like ginkgo trees for their *seeds*.
