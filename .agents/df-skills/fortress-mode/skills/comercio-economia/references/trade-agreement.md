# Trade agreement

> Fonte: [Trade agreement](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Trade_agreement) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Trade agreements** are made with the dwarven and human civilization by meeting the liaison or treasurer, respectively.

Existing trade agreements can be checked via the ivilizations screen: arrow down to highlight the civilization you wish to view, enter, and tab until the two agreements are displayed. Enter again on either to view.

## Duration

Trade agreements will disappear from the Civilizations screen when the liaison appears on-screen to provide space for the next year's agreement. Unfortunately, this means that, to consult the trade agreements prior to hauling goods over, it is necessary to store an offline copy or to very accurately predict the arrival of the traders.
