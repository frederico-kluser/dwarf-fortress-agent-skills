# Book binding

> Fonte: [Book binding](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Book_binding) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

A **book binding** is made from stone or wood at a craftsdwarf's workshop by a stonecrafter or woodcrafter, metal bars at the metalsmith's forge by a metalsmith, or glass at a glass furnace by a glassmaker.

Book bindings are stockpiled under Finished Goods \> Tools.

A written-on quire can be bound into a book by combining it with thread and a book binding. Bookbinding is done in the craftsdwarf's workshop by a dwarf with the bookbinding labor.
