# Future of the Fortress

> Fonte: [Future of the Fortress](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Future_of_the_Fortress) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Future of the Fortress** is the name of a series of threads on the Bay 12 Forums. Toady will periodically get around to a "Future of the Fortress reply", where he will answer any unanswered questions. Other forum members answer questions if the answers can be found elsewhere or the question is a repeat.

## History

Future of the Fortress threads started out as discussions of the development logs, called "Future of the Fortress" at the time. They eventually changed into a question and answer format.
