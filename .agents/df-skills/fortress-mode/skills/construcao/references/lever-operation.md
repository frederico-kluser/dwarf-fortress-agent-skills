# Lever operation

> Fonte: [Lever operation](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Lever_operation) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

Dwarves with the **lever operation** labor enabled (default) will pull levers with a ull job queued. Limiting this labor to only dwarves that will be near the lever (such as in a Burrow) is important to avoid having the job assigned to a dwarf hundreds of squares from the lever in a crisis.
