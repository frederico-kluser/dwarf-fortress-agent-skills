# Pump operator

> Fonte: [Pump operator](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Pump_operator) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Pump operator** is the skill related to the **pump operating** labor. Pump operators use screw pumps to pump liquids manually. In order to use the pump, simply uery the pump, and hit to "Start pump manually". When you want the pumping stopped, simply repeat the process.

This is useful for starting a perpetual motion power plant, or to move liquid on short notice and with little investment in machinery. However, it's not a good idea to put fortress-critical pumps on manual labor, as pump operation requires continuous dwarven labor.
