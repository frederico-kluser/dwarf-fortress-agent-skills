# Mussel

> Fonte: [Mussel](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Mussel) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Mussels** are a source of food for fortresses located near any body of water, except murky pools. They are also one of the few types of creatures, along with oysters, cave lobsters, nautiluses, and turtles, that are sources of shells. They can't be butchered, but can be cleaned at a fishery, which generates a shell and a prepared mussel, or cooked live as a raw mussel, which loses the shell.

Mussels are hateable vermin and will give unhappy thoughts to dwarves who absolutely detest mussels when they encounter them.

Some dwarves like mussels for their *beauty*.
