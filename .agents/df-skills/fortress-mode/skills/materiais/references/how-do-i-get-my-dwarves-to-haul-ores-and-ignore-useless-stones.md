# How do I get my dwarves to haul ores and ignore useless stones

> Fonte: [How do I get my dwarves to haul ores and ignore useless stones](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/How_do_I_get_my_dwarves_to_haul_ores_and_ignore_useless_stones) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

What you want to do is create a stone stockpile that doesn't allow normal stones to be carried to it.

- When you have the stockpile menu open , select *Custom stockpile settings* .
- Scroll down to Stone and nable it.
- You will see three categories appear in the middle column, 'Metal Ores', 'Economic stones', and 'Other'.
- Scroll across to the stone categories and orbid 'Economic stones' and 'Other'.
- Now exit out of the settings menu, and you will be able to place a stockpile that will only store 'Metal Ores'.

### Unique ores

You can choose to create a stockpile that only stores one kind of ore - for instance, just copper nuggets.

- Forbid 'Metal Ores'.
- Now scroll across to Metal Ore types.
- Now scroll down to 'Copper nuggets' and press  - you will notice that both 'Copper nuggets' and 'Metal Ores' becomes a lighter shade of grey. Note you can have more than one type of ore selected if you wish.
- Exit out of the menu and place the stockpile.

### Changing stockpile settings

You can alter a stockpile's settings at any time by using the examine buildings menu . Simply hover over it and you will see a number of options come up on the right hand window, including 'stockpile settings' . This will take you to the same menu as you saw earlier.
