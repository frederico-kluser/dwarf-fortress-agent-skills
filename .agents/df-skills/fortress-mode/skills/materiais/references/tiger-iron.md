# Tiger iron

> Fonte: [Tiger iron](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Tiger_iron) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Tiger iron** is an unremarkable low-value gem found in sedimentary layers.

## In Real Life

Tiger irons are related to the tigereye and cat's eye gems, where iron striations in the stone give the appearance of a tiger's stripes.

Tigereisen hg.jpg\|Tiger iron. Tiger's_eye_gems.jpg\|Processed and cut tiger iron gems.

Despite their name, tiger irons cannot be used to make tigers or iron, nor have they been documented removing tigers from wheels.
