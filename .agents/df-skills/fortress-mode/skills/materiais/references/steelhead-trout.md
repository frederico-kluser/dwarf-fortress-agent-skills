# Steelhead trout

> Fonte: [Steelhead trout](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Steelhead_trout) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Steelhead trout** are a type of aquatic vermin found in nearly all temperate rivers and oceans year-round. They are also found in arctic oceans. Steelhead trout are a ready source of food when cleaned at a fishery.

Some dwarves like steelhead trout for their *coloration*.
