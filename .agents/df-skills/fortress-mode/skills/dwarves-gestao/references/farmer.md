# Farmer

> Fonte: [Farmer](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Farmer) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Farmer** is a profession used for the production and use of plants, meat, plant fiber cloth, and beekeeping products. A dwarf whose highest skill is in this category is known as a Farmer.
