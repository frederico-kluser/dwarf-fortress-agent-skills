# Conversationalist

> Fonte: [Conversationalist](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Conversationalist) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Conversationalist** is a social skill used by dwarves while making friends. Dwarves that are particularly good at this tend to make friends easily and will often rise to mayoral positions.

To train this skill, let dwarves idle and chatter amongst themselves. Reserved dwarves will never gain experience in this skill.
