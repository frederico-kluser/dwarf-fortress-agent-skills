# Warlord

> Fonte: [Warlord](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Warlord) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

Some bandit gangs and criminal organizations call their boss a **warlord**. In fortress mode, you can eventually get the announcement popup that one of your dwarves or permanent residents, being the rightful heir, has inherited the title of Warlord of such a group. In legends mode, these groups are just called nomadic groups.
