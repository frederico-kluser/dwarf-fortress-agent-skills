# Cherry

> Fonte: [Cherry](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Cherry) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Cherry** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of aboveground trees, cherry wood is brown and produces brown products.

They of course produce cherries, which may be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into cherry wine. In the Summer season, cherry trees are unique in that they produce white clouds of cherry blossoms around themselves as they shed their flowers, which can easily be mistaken for mist, syndrome-causing clouds, or a cave-in. In autumn, the trees drop their fruit en masse.

Some dwarves like cherry trees for their *blossoms* and their *fruit*.
