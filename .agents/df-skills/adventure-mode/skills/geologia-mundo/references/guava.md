# Guava

> Fonte: [Guava](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Guava) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Guava** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, guava wood is brown and produces brown products.

Guava trees bear **guavas** that can be harvested by setting up a gathering zone. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into guava wine.

Some dwarves like guava trees for their *fruit*.
