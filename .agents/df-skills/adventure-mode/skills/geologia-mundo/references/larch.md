# Larch

> Fonte: [Larch](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Larch) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

The **Larch** is a type of above ground tree. It is found in temperate forests and occasionally in taiga regions. Like the overwhelming majority of overland trees, larch wood is brown and produces brown products.

Some dwarves like larches for their *cones* and their *needles*.

With proper instruction, the larch can be recognised from quite a long way away.
