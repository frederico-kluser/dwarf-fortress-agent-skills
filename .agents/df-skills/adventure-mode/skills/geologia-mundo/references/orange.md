# Orange

> Fonte: [Orange](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Orange) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Orange** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Contrary to its name, orange wood is in fact brown, and produces brown products.

Orange trees bear **oranges** that can be harvested by setting up a gathering zone.

Some dwarves like orange trees for their *fruit*.
