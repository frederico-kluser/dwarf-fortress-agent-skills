# Date palm

> Fonte: [Date palm](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Date_palm) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Date palms** are one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, date palm wood is brown and produces brown products.

Date palms bear **dates** that can be harvested by setting up a gathering zone. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into date wine. The flower stalks can also be harvested in the spring, but can only be eaten or cooked. Due to their narrow profile and the height that they grow to, date palms may not be worth harvesting unless nothing else is available.

Some dwarves like date palms for their *fruit*.
