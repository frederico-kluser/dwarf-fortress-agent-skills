# Custard-apple

> Fonte: [Custard-apple](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Custard-apple) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Custard-apple** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, custard-apple wood is brown and produces brown products.

Custard-apple wood is fairly lightweight (fifth-lightest, slightly less dense than willow).

Custard-apple trees bear custard-apples that can be harvested by setting up a gathering zone. They can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into delicious custard-apple cider.

Some dwarves like custard-apple trees for their *fruit*.
