# Swordfish

> Fonte: [Swordfish](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Swordfish) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Swordfish** are a species of large fish who can be found in temperate and tropical oceans. They are quite large, over 10 times heavier than a dwarf, and as such provide a great amount of returns when butchered. As non-vermin fish, swordfish can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with the use of a drowning chamber. Infant swordfish are called *swordfish fry*.

Some dwarves like swordfish for their *sharp snout*.
