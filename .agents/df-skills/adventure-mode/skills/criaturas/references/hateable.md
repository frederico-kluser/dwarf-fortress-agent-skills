# Hateable

> Fonte: [Hateable](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Hateable) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Hateable** vermin (creatures with the token) can appear as negative dwarven preferences. Dwarves who encounter detested vermin (or even an artistic rendering of one) may experience a negative thought. Being hated by some dwarves does not prevent the vermin from appearing as a positive preference for other dwarves. Be careful when selecting engravers - a fortress full of hated engravings can lead to civil unrest.
