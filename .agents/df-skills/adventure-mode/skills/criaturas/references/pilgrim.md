# Pilgrim

> Fonte: [Pilgrim](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Pilgrim) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Pilgrim** is a profession for some inhabitants of generated sites, like towns. Some pilgrims show up as visitors in fortress mode, and agents sometimes try to pose as pilgrims. Pilgrims are shown in white.

## World gen

In the world, a historical figure that has become a pilgrim will go on a pilgrimage. Pilgrimages consist of said pilgrim visiting sites with temples dedicated to their god, and praying inside the temple. Pilgrims will sometimes become prophets as a result of these pilgrimages.

## Fortress mode

As visitors, pilgrims will be attracted by temples open to visitors.

Pilgrims will not migrate to your fortress in this version.
