# Leatherworker

> Fonte: [Leatherworker](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Leatherworker) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Leatherworker** is the skill associated with the **leatherworking** labor. Leatherworking is used to make all of the leather goods at a Leather works. It is also used to sew leather images onto objects.

The leatherworker skill is also used to make leather craft goods at a craftsdwarf's workshop and armor at a leather works.
