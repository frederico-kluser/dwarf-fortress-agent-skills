# Spores

> Fonte: [Spores](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Spores) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

"**Spores**" is an informal player term not found in the game, but often used on the forums. It can refer to one of two things:

:\* Spores from underground rivers (or caverns) that allow underground shrubs and trees to grow in muddy areas.

:\* In versions before v0.40.19, the ability of DF dwarves and creatures to "breed by spores".
