# Structure

> Fonte: [Structure](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Structure) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Structures** are constructed or erected buildings found in sites. Structures acquire a history that can be viewed in Legends mode.

### Types

- Catacombs
- Dungeon
- Keep
- Library
- Market
- Mead hall
- Sewer
- Tavern
- Temple
- Tomb
- Underworld spire
