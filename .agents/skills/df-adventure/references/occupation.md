**Occupations** are positions in certain locations that can be assigned to dwarves. To assign a dwarf to an occupation, select the zone (either via z **zones** and selecting the zone, or from the P **place information menu** ) and then view the locations details with . You will see a list of available occupations in that location, and can assign or remove dwarves. In the selection menu, dwarves will be ordered by skills relevant to that occupation. You can assign multiple dwarves with the same occupation at a location, but any dwarf who gets assigned to an occupation at a location is automatically removed from any other occupation the dwarf might hold (meaning a dwarf can hold at most one occupation at a time).
