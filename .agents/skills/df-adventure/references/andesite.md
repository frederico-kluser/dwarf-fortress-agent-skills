# Andesite

> Fonte: [Andesite](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Andesite) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Andesite** is an unremarkable igneous extrusive stone. It can form whole layers, and is functionally identical to dacite and rhyolite. It is not magma-safe.
