# Steam Trading Cards

> Fonte: [Steam Trading Cards](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Steam_Trading_Cards) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

*Dwarf Fortress* premium edition features **Steam Trading Cards**, which are digital collectibles exclusive to Steam, and can be combined into badges, which contribute to your Steam profile level. They have been introduced into the game on December 22, 2022 alongside the v50.04 update.

There are 9 cards in the set. Playing the game will earn you 5 random cards, and upgrading the badge will require you to trade or buy additional cards.

## Badges

After completing a set of cards, you can combine them to upgrade your *Dwarf Fortress* badge, which can be showcased on your Steam profile. Badges level 1 through 5 feature a dwarf on tiles of wood, stone, bronze, silver, and gold tiles respectively, and the foil badge features an image of a gorlak on an adamantine tile.

## Booster Pack

Booster Packs are randomly given out to an eligible user. You become eligible once you have gotten all of your drops for playing the game. A Booster Pack contains 3 random cards from the set. You can also buy and trade the Booster Pack or purchase it using Gems, a Steam-exclusive digital currency.
