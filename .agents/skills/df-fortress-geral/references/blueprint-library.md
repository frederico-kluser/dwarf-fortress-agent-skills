## Automated Desalination Plant

Removes salt from water, producing fresh water by pumping salt water through a screw pump to a container made of constructed walls and floors with a windmill powering the pump and ramps for easy access to water.

## Building Destroyer... Destroyer

Trap designed to affect building destroyer creatures. A support holding up a constructed floor causes a cave-in, which will harm the creature. You will need pressure plates or some other trigger, as well as a building as bait, as building destroyers will NOT target or destroy supports.
