The **soundtrack** for *Dwarf Fortress* Premium spans two albums, as well as a series of interludes.

## Development

The Classic soundtrack for *Dwarf Fortress* was composed and played by the game's co-creator Toady One. The soundtrack consists of two flamenco-inspired[1] compositions. Tabs for both compositions can be found in DF2014:Soundtrack.

In 2022, the first *Dwarf Fortress* Premium edition OST was released. The main composers and performers are Omar Dabbous, Simon Swerwer, and Águeda Macias. Swerwer has previously released original *Dwarf Fortress*-inspired albums for SoundSense. With the launch of 51.01 in 2024, a new Adventurer mode-centered album was released.

A number of songs include lyrics in the Dwarven language, such as Drink & Industry, Bruising The Fat *(Shar Ikal)*, and Sisters of War, among others.

## Adventure mode OST

The Adventure mode OST features dynamic mixing, with pads, vocals, percussion, and the lead guitar able to duck in and out depending on the situation. The soundtrack release (and thus, the versions of these tracks that play in fortress mode) features those layers "baked" as the composers intended.
