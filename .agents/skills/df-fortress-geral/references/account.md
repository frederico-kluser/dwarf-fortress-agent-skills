# Account

An **account** is an abstract number that traces the amount of wealth of a given entity (companies, guilds, etc.) or historical figure. Although the game doesn't (yet) have a proper economy, the account system gives some targets for corruption mechanisms such as embezzlement (which shifts 'funds' from a site to a person) or sabotage (which abstractly reduces a site's funds). In addition, wealthy historical figures with a large account may grab houses or towers in towns. Mercenary companies also draw funds from their account to upgrade their weapons, and may disband if they go broke.
