Dwarf cancels task: Could not find path.

This message obviously means that the dwarf in question could not find a path to the destination for the task. This can be caused by closed and forbidden doors, floodgates, raised or retracted drawbridges, or any other physical obstruction of terrain-related nature, such as walls or water.
