# Siege engineer

> Fonte: [Siege engineer](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Siege_engineer) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Siege Engineer** is the skill associated with the **siege engineering** labor. Siege engineers make ballista and catapult parts in a siege workshop, and assemble those parts into stationary siege weapons, to be loaded and fired by siege operators.
