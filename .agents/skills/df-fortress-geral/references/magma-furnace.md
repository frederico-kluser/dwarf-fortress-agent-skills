# Magma furnace

> Fonte: [Magma furnace](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Magma_furnace) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

**Magma furnace** can refer to:

- Magma forge (to turn bars into items)
- Magma glass furnace (to create glass items)
- Magma kiln (for Pottery, and to create pearlash from potash)
- Magma smelter (to melt ores into bars of metals)
