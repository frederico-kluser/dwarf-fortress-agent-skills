The **mechanic's workshop** is a workshop which produces mechanisms out of stone\*. It is also used to construct traction benches.

*(\* For mechanisms out of metal, a mechanic must use a metalsmith's forge or magma forge.)*

## Menu

**Mechanic's Workshop**

- Make rock mechanisms
- Make traction bench

## Common Uses

- Make Mechanisms for simple traps, levers, pressure plates, and to link such triggers to doors, bridges, floodgates, cages, more complex traps, etc., etc., etc.
- Make Traction benches to equip hospital zones.

## Notes

- Traction benches require:
  - a Rope (produced in Clothier's shop), or a Chain (produced in Metalsmith's forge)
  - a Table
  - a Mechanism
- For non-dwarf fortresses, the name of this workshop will change to match if your fortress's primary race provides a custom value for the `[``PROFESSION_NAME``:MECHANIC]` token.
