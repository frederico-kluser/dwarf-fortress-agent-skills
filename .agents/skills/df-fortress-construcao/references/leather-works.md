**Leather works** is a workshop which produces leather items out of tanned hides made at the tanner's shop. These items include armor, backpacks, waterskins, quivers for military dwarves, and clothes and bags for civilian use. Leather armor is significantly less protective than metal armor, but is lighter and thus better for initial armor wearing training and for squads that value movement speed over defensive ability.

Decorating finished goods with leather is also done at this workshop.

Crafts made from leather are made at the craftsdwarf's workshop.

To create differently sized clothing or armor, request it to be made from the workshop as usual. Afterwards, go back to the main workshop menu and, by  button, set the details of the issued job. Click specify size, search for a species (e.g. humans), and click their name in the list.

Oldest leather tannery on Earth.
