# Cave swallow

> Fonte: [Cave swallow](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Cave_swallow) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Cave swallows** are a species of unremarkable vermin which can be encountered in caverns. They can be captured by trappers and turned into low-value pets.

Some dwarves like cave swallows for their *coloration*.
