*For the skill and labor, see Bookbinder.*

A **book binding** is made from stone, wood, metal or glass. They are made at a craftsdwarf's workshop using stone by a stonecrafter or wood by a woodcrafter , using metal bars at the metalsmith's forge by a metalsmith, or glass at a glass furnace by a glassmaker.

Book bindings are stockpiled under Finished Goods \> Tools.

A written-on quire can be bound into a book by combining it with thread and a book binding. Bookbinding is done in the craftsdwarf's workshop by a dwarf with the bookbinding labor.
