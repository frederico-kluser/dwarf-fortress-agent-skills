# Floor fungus

> Fonte: [Floor fungus](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Floor_fungus) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Floor fungus** is an underground floor-covering fungus, growing natively in the first cavern layer. As it is functionally a grass, grazers may feed on it.

As with the other underground grasses, floor fungus only appears after a cavern containing floor fungus has been discovered. After that has happened, it may spread on any below ground soil surface or on a muddied stone surface that has not been smoothed. The amount of mud on the surface does not affect the distribution or growth rate of subterranean plants 1.

While it might appear other types of fungi grows from the floor fungus, this is really just that they too grow on the same muddy floor.
