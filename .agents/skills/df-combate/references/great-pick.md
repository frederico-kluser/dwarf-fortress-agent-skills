# Great pick

> Fonte: [Great pick](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Great_pick) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

A **great pick** is an alternative to picks sized for large creatures. Troll, ogre, and blind cave ogre miners from dark goblin pits can arrive in sieges wielding this weapon.

A great pick is eight times the size of a standard pick - that is, it has the same internal attack properties, but hits with more force due to its size. A creature must be at least 225,000 cm³ to wield it, with some large species of animal people being the smallest creatures that can equip it in fortress mode.

When melted down, great picks produce roughly four bars of metal.
