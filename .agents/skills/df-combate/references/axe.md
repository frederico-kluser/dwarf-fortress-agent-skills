# Axe

> Fonte: [Axe](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Axe) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Axe** may refer to:

- Battle axe
- Great axe
- Training axe, a type of training weapon
- Stone axe, a tool craftable in Adventure Mode

Axes are weapons. Every axe except the training one can be used as equipment for tree cutting.
