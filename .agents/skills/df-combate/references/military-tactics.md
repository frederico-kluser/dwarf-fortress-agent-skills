**Military tactics** is a military skill. Along with leader and organizer skills, it affects mission simulations on the world map, giving the side with the better tactician a greater chance of victory.

Military tactics of the squad leader determines a squad's success chance in pillage and raze mission battles. The skill skews rolls in battles in opposition to the opponent's tactician skills. A successful pillaging mission where the squad gains a positional advantage over the enemy gives 500 experience points towards the tactician skill of the militia captain.

Military tactics is an available skill at embark. When assigning squad members, military tactician appears as a relevant skill for squad-leading nobles/appointments.
