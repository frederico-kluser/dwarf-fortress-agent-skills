A **gizzard** is the stomach found in many birds, such as the albatross. When butchered, it becomes a "prepared \ gizzard", which is an edible organ that can be eaten exactly like meat or other bodily organs. They have similar size to many other bodily organs, such as the brain or the liver.

Some creatures with gizzards also provide gizzard stones when butchered.
