# Jeweler

> Fonte: [Jeweler](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Jeweler) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Jeweler** is a category profession. It covers the cutting of rough gems, and the encrusting of cut gems onto finished goods, ammo, and furniture, in a jeweler's workshop. Graphically, they will wear lime green clothes.

A dwarf whose jewelry skills are even will be known as a Jeweler, otherwise by whichever skill is higher. Graphically, they wear lime green clothes, with brown underclothing, gloves and shoes.

The two Jeweler skills are:

|  |  |
|:--:|:--:|
|  | Jeweler |
|   | Gem cutter / Gem setter |
