# Jeweler

> Fonte: [Jeweler](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Jeweler) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Jeweler** is a category profession. It covers the cutting of rough gems, and the encrusting of cut gems onto finished goods, ammo, and furniture, in a jeweler's workshop.

A dwarf whose jewelry skills are even will be known as a Jeweler, otherwise by whichever skill is higher.
