# Liar

> Fonte: [Liar](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Liar) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Liar** is a social skill used by dwarves while socializing. Dwarves that are particularly good at this tend to make friends easily and will often rise to mayoral positions.

To train this skill, create a meeting area and let dwarves idle; dwarves idling near each other will chatter amongst themselves. Only dwarves that do not value the truth gain experience in this skill.
