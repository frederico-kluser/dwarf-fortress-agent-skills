**Presser** is the skill associated with the pressing labor. Pressers make oil, honey and craft materials at a screw press.

Pressing needs jugs (`[``TOOL_USE``:LIQUID_CONTAINER]`). Other storage items will not work as buckets and barrels are furniture.

Presser skill level is used to determine the speed at which the labor is completed. The type of container used also adds to the value of the final product.

Pressers produce an ingredient of soap and process honeycombs for a fortress as part of the soap industry and beekeeping industry respectively. Graphically, pressers will wear brown and dark purple clothing.
