**Fish dissector** is the skill associated with the fish dissection labor. Their only use is to make the mog juice extract from a captured moghopper at a fishery. Since moghoppers are fairly rare to catch, this skill is almost completely useless in its current state.

The fish dissector's skill level is used only to determine the speed at which the labor is completed. Since a liquid must be stored in a container (here, specifically a barrel), when traded the quality and material of the barrel used adds to the value of the final product.
