# Prophet

> Fonte: [Prophet](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Prophet) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Prophet** is a profession for some inhabitants of generated sites, like towns. Some prophets show up as visitors in fortress mode, and agents sometimes try to pose as prophets. In world generation, prophets will attempt to establish and expand religions. Prophets are shown in white.

## Fortress mode

Prophets will not migrate to your fortress in this version.
