# Consoler

> Fonte: [Consoler](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Consoler) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Consoler** is a social skill used by dwarves while conversing. Dwarves that are particularly good at this tend to make friends easily and will often rise to mayoral positions.

To train this skill, let dwarves idle and chatter amongst themselves. Chaotic dwarves, cruel dwarves, stoic dwarves, and dwarves unswayed by emotions will never gain experience in this skill.

Consoler skill may be important when unhappy dwarves meet with the mayor.\[Verify\]
