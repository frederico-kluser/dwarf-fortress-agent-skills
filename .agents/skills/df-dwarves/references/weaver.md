**Weaver** is the skill associated with the **weaving** labor. Weavers use a loom to weave cloth from silk thread (from webs left by cave spiders, giant cave spiders, or phantom spiders), plant fiber thread (processed from rope reeds or pig tails), or yarn (spun from wool of shearable creatures). Unlike most intermediate goods, cloth has a quality value, and dwarves with high skill in weaving create higher-quality cloth. Collecting spider webs also requires and trains the weaving skill.

Because they visit the caverns often to gather webs, weavers are particularly susceptible to encountering dangerous wildlife, such as the giant cave spiders which create the webs they're gathering. Consider assigning physically stronger dwarves for the job and giving them some war dogs or something similar, so that they have a better chance of fighting off hostile cavern beasts.
