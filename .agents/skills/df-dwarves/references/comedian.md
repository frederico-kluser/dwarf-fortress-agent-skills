# Comedian

> Fonte: [Comedian](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Comedian) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Comedian** is a social skill used by dwarves while making friends. Dwarves that are particularly good at this tend to make friends easily and will often rise to mayoral positions.

To train this skill, let dwarves idle and chatter amongst themselves. Dwarves with no sense of humor cannot gain comedic skill. Also, dwarves who are disgusted by merrymaking can't gain this skill unless they also have an extremely humorous personality.
