**Wood burner** is the skill associated with the wood burning labor, where dwarves turn wood into either ash or charcoal at a wood furnace. Dwarves with high skill level accomplish these tasks more quickly. Typically, a wood stockpile and a bar/block stockpile are placed near the furnaces.

## How do I get dwarves to burn wood?

Wood burner is, somewhat tenuously, considered a farming-related job (because ash can be made into potash, which fertilizes fields) and thus the labor is filed under Farming/*Related* labor menu, and not furnace operating.
