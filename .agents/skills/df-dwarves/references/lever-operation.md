Dwarves with the **lever operation** labor enabled (default) will pull levers with a Pull job queued. Limiting this labor to only dwarves that will be near the lever (such as in a Burrow) is important to avoid having the job assigned to a dwarf hundreds of squares from the lever in a crisis.
