**Thresher** is the skill that dwarves use when given the **plant processing** labor. Not all plants can be "processed", only those that have a useful by-product. If a stack of plants is processed, that's it, it cannot then also be cooked, brewed or otherwise prepared, however the seeds may be planted or (if appropriate) processed.

"Processing plants" actually covers 4 separate, different jobs done at a farmer's workshop, depending on the plant being processed:

- **Process plants**: turns fibrous plants into the corresponding types of thread plus their seeds, or certain unusual plants into their growths plus seeds.

- **Process plants (to bag)**: turns quarry bushes into quarry bush leaves and seeds (rock nuts). Five leaves and one rock nut are produced per quarry bush in the stack.

- **Process plants (to barrel)**: turns sweet pods into dwarven syrup and produces sweet pod seeds. Each sweet pod in the stack produces five units of syrup.

- **Process plants (to vial)**: turns valley herbs into golden salve. Requires a glass vial. Currently, there is no use for this extract other than as an export good.

Furthermore the "Make Dye" from leafs (and bark) jobs at the dyer's shop use this skill.
