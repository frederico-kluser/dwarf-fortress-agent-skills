**Glazer** is the skill associated with the glazing labor. Glazers apply glaze to ceramic and stone containers, statues, and craft items at the kiln or magma kiln. Glazer skill level determines the quality of the glaze, as well as presumably the speed at which the labor is completed.

Glazers can make earthenware ceramics watertight to use in storing liquids for a fortress as part of the ceramic industry.
