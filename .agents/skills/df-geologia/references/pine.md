# Pine

> Fonte: [Pine](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Pine) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Pine trees** are a type of above ground tree that can be found in any coniferous forest. Like the overwhelming majority of overland trees, pine wood is brown and produces brown products.

Some dwarves like pines for their *cones* and their *needles*.
