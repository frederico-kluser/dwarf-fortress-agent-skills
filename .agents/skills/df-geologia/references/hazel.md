# Hazel

> Fonte: [Hazel](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Hazel) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Hazel** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, hazel wood is brown and produces brown products.

Some dwarves like hazel trees for their *nuts*.
