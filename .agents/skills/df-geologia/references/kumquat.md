# Kumquat

> Fonte: [Kumquat](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Kumquat) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Kumquat** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, kumquat wood is brown and produces brown products.

Some dwarves like kumquat trees for their *fruit*.
