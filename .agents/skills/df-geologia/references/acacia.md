# Acacia

> Fonte: [Acacia](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Acacia) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Acacia** is one of the many genera of trees found on the surface, found in most dry tropical biomes, with the exception of deserts.

Acacia wood is peach-colored, which is currently displayed in-game as brown, and thus produces brown products. It does not rate highly as a food source, as the seeds are not edible raw - only once cooked.

Some dwarves like acacias for their *thorns*.
