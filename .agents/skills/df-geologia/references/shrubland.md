# Shrubland

> Fonte: [Shrubland](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Shrubland) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

Morella Faya.

**Shrublands** are biomes covered in grass, additionally many other plants occur, very similarly to grasslands or savannas. These biomes contain fewer trees than forests, although they usually have enough to get by unless you need large amounts of wood for fuel. They usually have plenty of murky pools, and often have brooks or even rivers. They tend to have less ore than mountains, but as with any biome, you can find some if you dig down a few z-levels and look hard enough.

Shrublands come in temperate and tropical varieties.
