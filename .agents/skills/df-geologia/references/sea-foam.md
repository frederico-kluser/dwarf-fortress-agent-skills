**Sea foam** is a feature of beach maps that is temporarily produced when ocean waves reach the shore. Depending on its density, sea foam appears as `°`, `∞`, or `≈`.

In the look (k) view, sea foam appears in bright magenta, similar to other flows such as miasma, mist, and smoke.
