# Banana

> Fonte: [Banana](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Banana) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Banana trees** are one of the many genera of trees found above ground in tropical biomes. Unlike most trees, it produces no wood (*owing to the fact that this 'tree' is in truth an overstated) shrub*); though it does produce a well-known fruit, which can be eaten raw, cooked, or brewed into banana beer. As these fruit have seeds, they are likely the lesser known wild banana.

Some dwarves like banana trees for their *fruit*.

Banana peels are especially dangerous to wagons and are sometimes seen trailing behind them.
