# Company

> Fonte: [Company](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Company) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

Trade **companies** are mostly world-gen only entities focused on lucrative trade. Their counting houses can be found in towns. In worldgen, traveling merchants and companies piggy-back on the more abstract industry/trade simulation that links most sites. At their peak, the companies will form multiple trade outposts in other civs along the trade routes to facilitate more cross-civ link formation.

Trade companies are distinct from mercenary orders, although these are sometimes also called companies.
