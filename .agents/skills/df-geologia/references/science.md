## a.k.a. Science On Fire

A specific form of spading in which one or more dwarven test subjects are observed in laboratory habitat. Dwarves may also conduct such test; indeed the same dwarf performing ‼Science‼ may also *be* the ‼Science‼ Dwarven adventurers may also perform ‼Science‼, but if it's not a dwarf, it just isn't ‼Science‼

Not to be confused with science. ‼Science‼, in comparison to science, generally involves more magma, more ‼DWARVES‼ and more Fun.
