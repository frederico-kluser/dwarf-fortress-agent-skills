# Coffee

> Fonte: [Coffee](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Coffee) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Coffee** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, coffee wood is brown and produces brown products.

Coffee trees bear coffee cherry fruit. These fruits can be eaten or cooked and produce coffee beans when eaten. Coffee beans cannot be eaten or brewed into coffee. Nor can their cherries be brewed into booze. So feel free to cut each and every one of these useless elf houses.

Some dwarves like coffee for their *yummy cherries*.
