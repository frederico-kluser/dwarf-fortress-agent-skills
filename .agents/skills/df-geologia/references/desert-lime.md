# Desert lime

> Fonte: [Desert lime](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Desert_lime) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Desert lime** is one of the many genera of trees found above ground. Like the vast majority of above ground trees, desert lime wood is brown and produces brown products.

It does not actually grow in deserts.

Some dwarves like desert lime trees for their *fruit*.
