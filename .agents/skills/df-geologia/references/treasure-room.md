**Treasure rooms** are procedurally generated reward chambers inside specific sites (for example, labyrinths, mysterious dungeons, or vaults) and are always guarded by megabeasts or other overprotective creatures. They may contain artifacts (such as a primordial remnant) or masterfully crafted armor or weapons. You may freely take what you want in these rooms after you survive the encounter.
