# Structure

> Fonte: [Structure](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Structure) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Structures** are constructed or erected buildings found in sites. Structures acquire a history that can be viewed in Legends mode.

### Types

- Catacombs can be found in Dark Fortresses, Caves (rare), Fortresses, Hillocks (rare), Forest Retreats and Towns.
- Counting house can be found in Dark Fortresses (rare), Fortresses, Forest Retreats and Towns. Constructed by Merchant Companies.
- Guildhall can be found in Fortresses, Forest Retreats (only founded during the reign of a dwarven/human civ of a forest retreat) and Towns. Constructed by Guilds.
- Keep can be found in Dark Fortresses, Caves (rare), Fortresses, Forest Retreats and Towns.
- Library can be found in Fortresses and Towns, but - while not found so far - it cannot be currently ruled out that a Library might get constructed in a Forest Retreat or Dark Fortress (after a human/darven civilization did take over a site).
- Market can be found in Dark Fortresses, Caves (rare), Fortresses (all Fortresses, which are founded after year 1 during world-gen will construct a market as first building), Hillock (rare), Forest Retreats and Towns.
- Mead hall can be found in Dark Pits, Hillocks, Hamlets and Forest Retreats. If during world-gen a Dark Pit becomes a Dark Fortress or a Hamlet becomes a Town, then a previously constructed Mead Hall will cease to exist.
- Sewer can only be found in Towns.
- Tavern can be found in Dark Fortresses, Hillocks (rare), Fortresses, Forest Retreats and Towns.
- Temple can be found in Dark Fortresses, Caves, Hillocks, Fortresses, Forest Retreats, Towns, Monestaries and Forts. A Monestary will always have a temple.
- Tomb can be found in Dark Fortresses, Hillock (rare), Fortresses, Forest Retreats, Towns and Tombs (site).
- Tower "Tower (structure)") can only be found in Towns.
- Underworld spire can only be found in Dark Fortresses.

*Note:* Mountain Halls do not contain any structure.
