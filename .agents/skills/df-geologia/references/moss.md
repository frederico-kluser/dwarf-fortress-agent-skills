**Moss** is a special covering that appears on smooth stone outdoors. It is found primarily in shrines "Shrine (megabeast)") inhabited by bronze colossuses and titans. It is also sometimes found in the object testing arena\[Verify\]. It also is only visible when covering a wall (and only a wall) with premium graphics enabled.

Not to be confused with cave moss, a special type of grass that grows in subterranean soil.
