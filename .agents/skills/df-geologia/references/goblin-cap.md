# Goblin-cap

> Fonte: [Goblin-cap](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Goblin-cap) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Goblin-caps** are a type of underground tree found in the second and third levels of the caverns near the water, and bearing no relation to goblins. When cut down, they yield red logs suitable for producing colorful furniture.

Some dwarves like goblin-caps for their *stunning color*.
