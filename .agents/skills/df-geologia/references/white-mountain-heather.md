# White mountain heather

> Fonte: [White mountain heather](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/White_mountain_heather) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**White mountain heather** is a type of grass that grows exclusively in otherwise-inhospitable tundra and mountainous biomes. It is indistinguishable from most other types of grass.
