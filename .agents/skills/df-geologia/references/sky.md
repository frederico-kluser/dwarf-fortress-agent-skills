# Sky

> Fonte: [Sky](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Sky) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

*You may be looking for open space. Sky is open space (and appears as open space when looked at), but cannot exist underground.*

▓

**Sky** is represented by a mass of solid blue (cyan) tiles across the map. Sky only appears if there are two levels of successive open space below the current view, *and* there are no floors or constructions above the current view. A tile that has direct, vertical access to the sky (above the tile) is classified as outside (as opposed to inside). "Outside" tiles can cause sickness and vomiting in cave-adapted dwarves.
