Example zones tab, displayed after selecting P

The **place information menu** is accessed from the main view by clicking the P menu icon or pressing Shift - P. This allows you to view related information on zones, locations, stockpiles, workshops, farm plots and siege engines by selecting the corresponding tabs.

This menu has replaced the **building list screen** from v0.47 of the game.
