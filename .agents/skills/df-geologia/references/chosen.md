The **chosen** is an adventurer that receives aid and guidance from a patron deity. It is a successor to the Demigod (now known as Easy) difficulty mode.

## Gameplay

The Chosen destiny can be selected when creating an adventurer, as long as temples have been founded by playable civilizations. It functions as an in-world tutorial, providing guidance specific to each world.

At the beginning of a Chosen's adventure, their patron will instruct them to seek out a priest of the same religion. Talking to the priest allows the player to k Offer service and accept a quest to obtain a primordial remnant from a mysterious lair. To complete the quest, give the artifact to the priest.

## Abilities

Chosen adventurers start off as regular creatures without extraordinary abilities. Rarely, their patron will deflect an incoming attack via "divine intervention" as if shielded, while also stunning the creature, though the circumstances for this event are not well understood.
