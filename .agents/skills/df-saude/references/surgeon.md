# Surgeon

> Fonte: [Surgeon](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Surgeon) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Surgeon** is the skill associated with the Surgery labor, as well as the hospital occupation associated with it. Surgeons are responsible for treatment of complex overlapping injuries, damage to internal organs, and syndromes, using the tables and traction benches at a hospital, bringing your fortress's injured heroes back from the brink as part of the health care industry. The skill is also used by scholars when thinking about medical science-related topics.

Surgeon skill level is related to rate of success and post-operation infection. The patient must be at rest to perform surgery. If the patient is not at rest, the surgeon will cancel surgery. The patient will stop resting when they get too hungry or thirsty. The patient may cease resting while being carried to the surgery table, if it is a long distance away.

A job to remove necrotic tissue starts with a job, which appears to be a prep phase, followed by a job, after which another job occurs. The necrotic tissue is gone at the end of the job. The body parts from which the tissue was removed will be light yellow (oddly, even body parts that were previously darker due to existing injuries). The initial surgery job will be preceded by a job if possible.

## Bugs

This occupation is affected by the healthcare labors bug.
