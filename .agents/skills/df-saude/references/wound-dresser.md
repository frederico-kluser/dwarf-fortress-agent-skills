# Wound dresser

> Fonte: [Wound dresser](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Wound_dresser) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Wound dresser** is the skill associated with the dressing wounds labor in healthcare. Wound dressers are responsible for cleaning wounds and applying bandages after they have been sutured, for which they need clean water, soap and cloth (any kind of cloth will do).

Wound dresser skill level is related to the speed with which the labor is completed. Wound dressers try to stop the bleeding - oh Armok the bleeding - on your fortress' injured as part of the health care industry.
