# Language SYM.txt/Food

> Fonte: [Language SYM.txt/Food](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Language_SYM.txt/Food) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

## Usage

Legendary beings, such as a giant, associated with the spheres of agriculture or food will have words from this symbol in their name.

A noun from \[SYMBOL:FOOD\] is used as the symbol for naming a tavern, in the form "The \[ADJECTIVE\] \[FOOD\]" or "The \[FOOD\] of \[NOUN\]". The other words are defined by what symbols the entity has assigned to the OTHER context (often through REMAINING or ALL).
