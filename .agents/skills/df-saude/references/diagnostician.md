# Diagnostician

> Fonte: [Diagnostician](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Diagnostician) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Diagnostician** is the skill associated with the diagnosis labor, as well as the hospital occupation associated with it. Diagnosers are responsible for deciding how a dwarf who is hurt should be treated at a hospital.

Diagnostician skill level is related to the speed at which the labor is completed. It is the only relevant skill of the chief medical dwarf noble position.
