The **fuel industry** deals with the harvesting and use of wood or minerals for the production of fuel in the Metal industry, Glass industry, and Ceramic industry. It is considered a secondary industry because it refines raw harvested materials, and prepares them for further use in secondary industries.

## Products

- Coke
- Charcoal
