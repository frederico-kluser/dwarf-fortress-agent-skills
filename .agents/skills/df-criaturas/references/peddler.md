# Peddler

> Fonte: [Peddler](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Peddler) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Peddler** is a profession for some inhabitants of generated sites, like towns. Some peddlers show up as visitors in fortress mode, and agents sometimes try to pose as peddlers. Peddlers are shown in grey.

## Fortress mode

Peddlers will not migrate to your fortress in this version.
