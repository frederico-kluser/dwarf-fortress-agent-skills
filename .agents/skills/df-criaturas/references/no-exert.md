# No Exert

> Fonte: [No Exert](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/No_Exert) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**No Exert** creatures (those with the [`[NOEXERT]`](/index.php/Creature_token#NOEXERT "Creature token") token) need no sleep, and never become tired or exhausted. In the case of a particularly dangerous creature like a bronze colossus, the NOEXERT tag guarantees that the foe will continue the battle as long as there are any dwarves left alive.

\
