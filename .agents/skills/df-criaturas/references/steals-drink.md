# Steals drink

> Fonte: [Steals drink](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Steals_drink) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

Creatures with the [`[CURIOUSBEAST_GUZZLER]`](/index.php/Creature_token#CURIOUSBEAST_GUZZLER "Creature token") creature token will happily devour your alcohol stocks, if given the chance - this can prove particularly fun when your newly-founded fort's entire alcohol supply is still sitting topside in the wagon "Wagon (embark)").

This property can also be used to capture certain creatures (such as grizzly bears) by surrounding a small drink stockpile with cage traps.

## Creatures who can steal drinks

The following creatures possess the [`[CURIOUSBEAST_GUZZLER]`](/index.php/Creature_token#CURIOUSBEAST_GUZZLER "Creature token") token.

- Black bears
  - Black bear men
  - Giant black bears
- Dark gnomes
- Ettins
- Giants
- Giant moles
- Giant rats
- Grizzly bears
  - Grizzly bear men
  - Giant grizzly bears
- Large rats
- Mountain gnomes
- Naked mole dogs
- Polar bears
  - Polar bear men
  - Giant polar bears
- Sloth bears
  - Sloth bear men
  - Giant sloth bears
