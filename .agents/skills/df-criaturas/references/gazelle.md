# Gazelle

> Fonte: [Gazelle](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Gazelle) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Gazelles** are small animals who live in tropical savannas and grasslands, where they appear in herds of 5-10 individuals. They are benign herbivores, smaller even than dogs, who spend their time meandering about aimlessly, vulnerable to any hunter or squad who wants to make target practice out of them, and they'll rarely fight back if confronted. A newborn gazelle is named a *fawn*.

Gazelles can be captured in cage traps and trained into exotic pets. They can be butchered for products equivalents to a goat, which includes horns, hooves and teeth. Trained gazelles are grazers, and therefore require a constant source of grass to survive.

Some dwarves like gazelles for their *grace*.
