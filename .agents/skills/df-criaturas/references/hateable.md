xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Hateable** vermin (creatures with the [`[VERMIN_HATEABLE]`](/index.php/Creature_token#VERMIN_HATEABLE "Creature token") token) can appear as negative dwarven preferences. Dwarves who encounter detested vermin may experience a negative thought. Being hated by some dwarves does not prevent the vermin from appearing as a positive preference for other dwarves. Be careful when setting up your zoos - poor selections of creatures can lead to civil unrest.
