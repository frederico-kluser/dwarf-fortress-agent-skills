# Giant giraffe

> Fonte: [Giant giraffe](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Giant_giraffe) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Giant giraffes** are gargantuan versions of the giraffe, a long-necked grazer found roaming savage tropical savannas and shrublands. Being a giant creature, giant giraffes provide an absurd amount of (extremely valuable, being 5x the value of common domestic animals) butchering returns.

Giant giraffes also make decent exotic pets, but their real value lies as a staple of the meat industry.
