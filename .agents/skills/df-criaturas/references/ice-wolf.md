# Ice wolf

> Fonte: [Ice wolf](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Ice_wolf) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Ice wolves** are evil-aligned versions of the common wolf who can be found at evil tundras and glaciers. They are almost identical to their non-ice counterparts save for their coloration and for being 10,000 cm³ larger, coming close to the size of an adult dwarf. This makes them potentially dangerous to civilians, though an equipped military squad will make short work of them. Ice wolves possess a pet value of 50, but lack the necessary tokens to be trainable.

Some dwarves like ice wolves for their *piercing howls*.
