# Dwarf cancels fill pond: Inappropriate building

> Fonte: [Dwarf cancels fill pond: Inappropriate building](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Dwarf_cancels_fill_pond:_Inappropriate_building) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

This error occurs if a dwarf tries to fill a pond and the pond zone isn't over open space. To fix it, channel underneath the pond zone, or delete the pond zone and make another over open space.
