# Halibut

> Fonte: [Halibut](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Halibut) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Halibuts** are a species of fish who can be found in temperate and arctic oceans. Over three times heavier than a dwarf, they provide a good amount of returns when butchered. As non-vermin fish, halibuts can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with the use of a drowning chamber. Infant halibuts are called *halibut fry*.

Some dwarves like halibuts for their *fins*.
