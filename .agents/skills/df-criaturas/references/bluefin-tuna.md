# Bluefin tuna

> Fonte: [Bluefin tuna](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Bluefin_tuna) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Bluefin tuna** are a species of large fish who can be found in all types of ocean. Ten times the size of a dwarf, they provide a great amount of returns when butchered. As non-vermin fish, bluefin tuna can't be fished by fisherdwarves, but can be caught with the use of a drowning chamber. Infant bluefin tuna are called *bluefin fry*.

Some dwarves like bluefin tuna for their *finlets*.
