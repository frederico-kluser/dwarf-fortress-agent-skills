# Giant deer

> Fonte: [Giant deer](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Giant_deer) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

Despite their large size, **giant deer** are usually rather harmless, unless a dwarf ends up getting them cornered. They can be tamed, and have a decent pet value of 500 like most giant animals, but they need a large pasture to survive, and won't be useful for fortress defense.
