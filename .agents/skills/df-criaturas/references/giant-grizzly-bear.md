*A huge monster in the shape of a grizzly bear.*

\
Stronger versions of the regular grizzly bear, **giant grizzly bears** can be found in savage temperate biomes. They are capable of posing a threat to even a well-prepared fortress, and are best dealt with using cage traps. Once trapped, they can be trained for hunting and war by an animal trainer.

Not the huggable type.\
