# Beaching

> Fonte: [Beaching](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Beaching) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Beaching** is a characteristically odd behavior carried out by creatures with a [`[BEACH_FREQUENCY]`](/index.php/Creature_token#BEACH_FREQUENCY "Creature token") creature token. These aquatic animals will occasionally strand themselves on land, where they are immobile and unable to breathe. This is intended to model the poorly-explained real-life behavior of similar animals; see cetacean stranding on Wikipedia for more information.
