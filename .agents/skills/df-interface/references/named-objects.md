# Named objects

> Fonte: [Named objects](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Named_objects) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

Named objects are listed in the objects menu O under the Named objects tab. This lists any items in your fortress that have been made a symbol of office. These are **not** the same as artifacts, but artifacts may be chosen as a symbol of office. However, it seems that for the purposes of stockpile settings, any named objects will be stored as if they were artifacts.\[Verify\]
