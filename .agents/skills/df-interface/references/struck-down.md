# Struck down

> Fonte: [Struck down](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Struck_down) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

When the message "\[dwarf\] has been **struck down**" appears, some dwarf has received an immediately fatal injury in combat, namely: the brain being damaged, the head being chopped off, or the lower/upper body being cut in two. Too much of this, and your fort may experience large amounts of fun.

If a dwarf is not seen dying by another dwarf, there will be, obviously, no announcement of the dwarf dying.

More specific death messages for other scenarios include the following: shot and killed, burned to death, drowned, encased in ice, encased in obsidian, melted, crushed by a drawbridge, fell into a deep chasm, crushed by a cave-in, died after colliding with an obstacle, and, most commonly among nobles, killed by a spike.
