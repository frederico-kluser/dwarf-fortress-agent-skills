## In Real Life

Shale is a fine-grained sedimentary rock composed of mud that is a mix of flakes of clay minerals and tiny fragments (silt-sized particles) of other minerals, such as quartz and calcite. It metamorphises into slate.

One of the hardest forms of mud ever known.
