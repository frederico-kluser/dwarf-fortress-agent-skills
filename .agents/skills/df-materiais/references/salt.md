# Salt

> Fonte: [Salt](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Salt) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

*For the mineral, see Rock salt.*

**Salt** is a contaminant that makes oceanic water unsuitable for drinking.

Randomly generated creatures, such as forgotten beasts, may sometimes be composed either of salt (in powder form), or of solid salt.
