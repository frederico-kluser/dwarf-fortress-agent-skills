**Indigo tourmaline** is one of the higher-value, semi-precious gems that can be found within sedimentary and metamorphic stone layers, as well as within granite. Scoring a ×25 multiplier, the same as clear zircon, these are great for saving for later, when you have great gem-cutters and gem-setters, to get very high-value objects.
