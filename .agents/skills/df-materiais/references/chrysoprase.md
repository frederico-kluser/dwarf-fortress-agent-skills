**Chrysoprases** are unremarkable low-value gems found in sedimentary layers.

## In Real Life

Chrysoprase is the proper name for a green chalcedony, also known as Australian jade. It's relatively rare.

A chrysoprase coffer. It is encrusted with Pink tourmalines, Light yellow diamonds, and Clear diamonds and encircled with bands of gold. On the item is an image of flowers.
