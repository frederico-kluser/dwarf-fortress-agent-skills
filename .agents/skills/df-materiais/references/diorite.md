**Diorite** is an igneous intrusive, non-magma-safe stone which can form whole layers.

Because diorite does not contain a wide variety of large cluster and veins, it is an interesting layer for players who insist on color uniformity. Digging and carving inside a diorite layer will produce a uniformly dark gray area, with occasional gold veins and small gem clusters.
