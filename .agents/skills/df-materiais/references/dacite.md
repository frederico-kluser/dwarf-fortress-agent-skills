**Dacite** is one of the types of igneous extrusive stone that forms entire layers, functionally identical to andesite and rhyolite. It does, however, have a slightly different melting point, and is not magma safe.

In real life, this stone is similar to felsite, which is a month representing late spring in *Dwarf Fortress*.
