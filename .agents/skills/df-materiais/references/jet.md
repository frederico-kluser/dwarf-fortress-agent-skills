**Jet** is a non-economic dark purple stone found in large clusters in sedimentary layers. It is *not* magma-safe, and so should only be used where any other stone would suffice, aside from one notable property.

Jet is the most lightweight stone besides raw adamantine and lignite, ~40% lighter than the next lightest non-economic stone (Saltpeter). This property makes it preferable for making large pots and other commonly-hauled items, and absolutely *inappropriate* for stone-fall traps and catapults. Likewise, jet crafts are even more lightweight than normal crafts; this can be very helpful for maximizing caravan output.

## In Real Life

In real life, jet is actually a product of decaying wood under extreme pressure, and is an extremely low grade of lignite. In DF, however, jet is not a source of fuel. Since it is very soft and easily carved, historically it has been a popular source of jewelry and crafts.
