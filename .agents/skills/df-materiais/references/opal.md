# Opal

> Fonte: [Opal](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Opal) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Opal** is a broad category of ornamental (low-value) and semi-precious (mid-value) gems. The exact gem will be one of a couple dozen specific types of opal, such as bandfire opal, pipe opal or pineapple opal. All opals in *Dwarf Fortress* are listed below.

"Opal" is also the name of the eleventh month of the dwarven calendar, covering mid-winter.
