# Tin glaze

> Fonte: [Tin glaze](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Tin_glaze) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Tin glaze** is a type of glaze that can be applied to jugs, statues, large pots, and crafts made of stone or ceramics. It is applied to these items by a glazer at a kiln or magma kiln by using a chunk of cassiterite. Tin bars can't be used for this, so if you want to use tin glaze be sure not to smelt all of your cassiterite into tin.

Tin glaze has material value of 10, worth twice as much as ash glaze, likely partially due to its limited availability. Otherwise, it is functionally identical, making earthenware jugs and large pots waterproof and able to store liquids.
