# Unknown substance

> Fonte: [Unknown substance](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Unknown_substance) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

''Not to be confused with Unknown.

**Unknown substance** is a hardcoded material that takes the form of a light gray liquid. It freezes at [](temperature) and boils at [](temperature), and has the same density as water. It does not seem to appear in this version of the game, but it was well-known in v0.28.181.40d for appearing in glowing pits occupied by tentacle demons, along with both types of filth.
