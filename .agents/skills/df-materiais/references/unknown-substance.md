# Unknown substance

> Fonte: [Unknown substance](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Unknown_substance) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

!!UNKNOWN!!  · xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

*Not to be confused with Unknown.*

**Unknown substance** is a hardcoded material that takes the form of a light gray liquid. It freezes at 10000 °U  and boils at 10180 °U  , and has the same density as water. It was well-known in v0.28.181.40d for appearing in glowing pits occupied by tentacle demons, along with both types of filth.
