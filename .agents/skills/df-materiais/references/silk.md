# Silk

> Fonte: [Silk](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Silk) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL & MIT). Snapshot 2026-06.

xTATTEREDx  · +FINE+  · \*SUPERIOR\*  · ≡EXCEPTIONAL≡  · ☼MASTERWORK☼

**Silk** cloth is made from silk thread. Silk thread can be gathered from the webs of brown recluse spiders, brown recluse spider men, cave spiders, giant cave spiders, and phantom spiders (as well as any web-spinning forgotten beasts, titans, or Hidden Fun Stuff), which is then woven into cloth at a loom. Webs are automatically collected by any worker who has the weaving labor activated and access to a loom, which automatically produces the work task "collect webs" and turns collected webs directly into silk thread. No stockpile is necessary for this collection.

Automatic collection of webs can be disabled from the orders menu (Accessed through the Labor Menu y) - this is useful if the accessible webs are located in a dangerous area or if you want to temporarily free up your weavers for other tasks.

Silk was once prized as a superior choice for clothing since it was surprisingly protective for a non-metal material; this is no longer true. It's still better than plant fiber as the latter is more than thrice as heavy, and it is marginally stronger. All kinds of silk have the same performance; the only advantage of Giant Cave Spider silk is its higher value multiplier.

Nerve-ending friendly.
