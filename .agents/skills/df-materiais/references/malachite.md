**Malachite** is one of several ores of copper. It is found as veins, is rare compared to other copper ores, and is among the few types of stone that are not fire-safe.

Other copper ores include native copper and tetrahedrite.

"Malachite" is the name of the 5th month of the dwarven calendar, covering mid-Summer.

## In real life

Malachite is a hydrated copper carbonate (Cu2(CO3)(OH)2) that forms as a result of the weathering of pre-existing copper deposits. Malachite is known to form alongside both Chrysocolla, and Azurite (which is not present in *Dwarf Fortress*) which it commonly pseudomorphs from.
