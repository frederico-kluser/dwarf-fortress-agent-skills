An alluvial layer of land in Canyon State Park.

Several minerals, ores, and gems are specified to occur at least in part within **alluvial layers**. Alluvial stone is loose and unconsolidated stone that has been eroded away, smoothed down by water flow, and then redeposited in a non-marine setting. In Dwarf Fortress, all sedimentary layers are capable of containing alluvial deposits.

Whether or not a particular stone, gem, or ore appears in the alluvial layer is controlled by the `[ENVIRONMENT:ALLUVIAL:...]` tag in its raw files. Alluvial environments can contain clusters of:

- native gold
- native platinum
- cassiterite
- jade gems (blue, lavender, pink, white, and green)
