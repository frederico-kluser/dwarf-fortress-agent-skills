**Amber opals** are ornamental-quality gems that can be found in small clusters in any stone. Among opals, it is not very valuable; bone, cherry, gold, jasper, milk, moss, onyx, pineapple, pipe, prase, resin, shell, wax, and wood opals are all equivalent in value.
