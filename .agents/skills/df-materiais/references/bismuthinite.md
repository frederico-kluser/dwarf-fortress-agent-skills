# Bismuthinite

> Fonte: [Bismuthinite](https://dwarffortresswiki.org/index.php/Bismuthinite) — Dwarf Fortress Wiki (GFDL/MIT)

**Bismuthinite** is the only ore of bismuth, which is used to create bismuth bronze. It is generally found in small clusters, and is quite rare in comparison to other ores.
